Lore Page

Prismplex allows players to create their own lore, leaving most of the story of the server up to the community to imagine. This page will soon have two main things: First, there will be an overview of the main server lore so that people can learn the general story of the server and its various servers, and use that as a jumping off point to create their own characters and stories if they would like! Second, this page will also be a showcase of player-made characters, stories, videos, art, and more!

The #lore room in the Discord is also a great place to look for lore! Important things are pinned in that room, and it allows for a space for players to showcase and discuss the lore related things they are working on! Players who are actively involved in the lore will also receive the Loremaster role.
