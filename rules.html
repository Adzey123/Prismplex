Prismplex Rules

Rules are key to maintaining Prismplex as a friendly community.

We aim to have the rules listed on this page to be as clear as we can so that they are easy to understand for everyone, including neurodivergent people and other accessibility needs.
The rules may change. This page was last updated 8th January 2024

Sections

The rules are split into sections depending on what part of our network they apply to.
  • General Behaviour - These rules are general for the entirety of Prismplex, including in the Discord server and in-game
  • Discord Specific - These are rules specific to inside our Discord server
  • In Game - These are rules to follow in-game wherever you end up on our network of Minecraft servers
  • Individual servers - rules specific to individual servers on our network for cases where different rules may apply to different servers for reasons such as gameplay style
    • Azure SMP - Rules to follow on the Azure SMP Minecraft server

General Behaviour

These rules apply to anywhere inside the Prismplex community, including the Discord server and Minecraft servers. They are just a basic guideline of rules to follow to be a nice person.

  • Be Friendly. Please be nice to others, don't be Racist, Homophobic, Transphobic, Ableist or anything else. We have a diverse community with people of different genders, sexualities, races, religions, ages, and abilities, and it is key to be respectful to others by listening to others, not pushing them down, and having respectful discussions when needed.
  • Don't break the rules of apps we use. This includes Discord and Minecraft by following the Discord TOS and Minecraft EULA This includes age limits of being at least 13 years old (or whatever your country's laws are) and rules around unsafe or regulated content for Discord and not distributing or using hacked clients and not harassing people, using hate speech, encouraging illegal activity and more as covered in the Minecraft EULA .
  • No promotion or advertising unless approved. Sharing your content from sites such as YouTube or Twitch and other smaller self-promotion is okay as long as it is in relevant channels, but sharing other Discord servers and other advertising (not including advertising about in-game matters on our Minecraft servers) is not allowed without approval from staff. Self-Promotion from new members that have not yet engaged with the community may not be allowed.

  • Keep stuff PG. Keep the server clean, follow common sense and assume everyone you talk to is under 18.
  • Don't use alt accounts to bypass punishments. Alt accounts are allowed but if you get punished, such as being banned, using an alt account to bypass this is not allowed.
  • Don't be Inappropriate. This includes discussing sexual topics in chats or harassment of someone, especially with non-age-appropriate materials.
  • Report stuff. Please report rule breakers, bugs, or issues and more; this assists us in running our server and means that issues can be solved. To report a staff member, contact a higher-ranking staff member such as Moderator, Admin, or Owner.
  • Listen to Staff. Listen to members of the staff team. This includes accepting decisions made. Feedback and suggestions are allowed but please phrase in a reasonable way.
  • Staff have the final say. Our staff team is able to enforce the rules and decide what breaks them and what doesn't. If you want to discuss an action made by a staff member, reach out to a higher-ranked staff member.

Discord Specific

These are the rules to follow in our Discord server.

  • Don't Spam. Don't spam chats in Discord, please.
  • You must be at least 13 to be in the Discord server. As required by Discord TOS and laws in many countries, users of Discord must be at least 13. Players under 13 can play our server in-game with supervision but cannot be in the Discord server.
  • Censor sensitive topics. Please use spoiler tags to censor sensitive topics and provide content warnings for sensitive subjects; you can surround text with '||' on each side to spoiler tag it in Discord, e.g. || this message is spoilered ||
  • Use the #Real_Talk channel appropriately. The real talk space is a channel for more serious conversations. Joking with things posted in the channel is not allowed. Topics that may be triggering or topics that are more serious should be contained into the real talk channel. Please be respectful and consider others when using the channel.

In Game

These rules apply in-game on Minecraft across all our servers.

  • Don't Spam. Once again, don't spam in-game chats unnecessarily.
  • Follow Minecraft EULA. You are playing Minecraft, you must follow Minecraft's rules.
  • No hacking and No using Mods for an unfair advantage. Using hacks such as X-ray, seed-crackers, PvP advantage mods, fly hacks, and more are strictly not allowed. Other game Mods that are allowed depend on the server, but staff will always have the final say. If you are unsure if a mod is allowed or not, ask a staff member.
  • Do not harass other players. Don't target other players just to harass them; this includes stalking chat messages and Dynmap for the purpose of harassing others.

Azure SMP

These rules are specifically for playing on the Azure SMP.

  • No Griefing or Stealing. While we do have a land claim plugin, griefing and stealing are still not allowed, and staff have the ability to see logs of who did what. This is to ensure the server stays peaceful.
  • Report bugs and issues. To ensure the server works properly, please report any bugs or issues you encounter or hear about into the bug reporting channel or by messaging a high-ranking staff member. Rewards may be given to players who report bugs.
  • Mayor elections and Azure Mayor
    • Elections for a mayor will be run by staff members around every two months.
    • The mayor will create laws for players to follow in the town; staff will step in if needed. The mayor may have staff assist in enforcing their laws when reasonable and required, but punishment is up to the mayor.
    • When a rigged election or other lore event is involved with the mayor, it will be made clear to players.
  • Lore
    • The Azure SMP has lore created by players; it should be optional to participate in, and major events should have a neutral staff member be informed.
    • Staff members may participate in lore, and their actions in lore, such as a war, are not related to their rank as a staff member.
  • Don't Hack. Don't use hacks such as X-ray, Seedcrackers, PvP mods, and fly hacks.
  • Don't Use Mods that Give an Unfair Advantage. We allow mods for things such as build schematics, minimaps, accessibility, and similar. But mods that provide an advantage in gameplay or hacks are not allowed. Staff members have the final say on what is allowed and what isn't; if you are unsure, ask a staff member first.

Enforcement and Punishments

To enforce the rules, staff members have access to logs to track player actions and the ability to punish as reasonable.

  • Timeouts Players may receive a timeout or mute of up to a week, which removes their ability to use chats. This is often a first warning for behavior that is not appropriate, such as starting arguments or causing unnecessary drama; it will also be used to control spam when needed.
  • Limiting Role For offenses relating to misuse of channels, such as looking_for_friends and real_talk, a role may be added to the player, removing their access to these channels.
  • Temp-Ban In some cases, a player may receive a temporary ban from the Minecraft servers; this is often until the situation is discussed with other staff members to find an alternative or as a set time to punish the player.
  • Ban Players may be permanently banned from the Minecraft and Discord servers for bad behavior and breaking the rules. Most of the time, prior warnings would have been made, or sometimes the behavior was bad enough or indicating a troll or someone who is not likely to change in the future.
  • In-Game Fine For minor in-game offenses, a player may be fined in-game money as reasonable; alternatively, items may be taken or other forms of punishment in-game.

Punishments may be disputed by contacting a high-ranking staff member (Mod, Admin, or Owner) or through email to minecraft.prism.server@gmail.com


Something Not Here?

Please contact a staff member to discuss. It is hard to consider all scenarios when creating the rules for a server. These rules act as a base version but things not specified may still be punished, and the rules edited.

I Need Help!

If you need help reporting someone or if you are being targeted by another player, please contact a staff member. If it would help, choose a trusted friend on the server to help you reach out. Our staff team is here to help when needed, and don't be scared to message to just ask.


Behavior such as sexual targeting of minors (under 18) is not allowed, especially if the other person is an adult. This is illegal in most places around the world and should be discussed and reported; do not be scared to reach out for help.

Events outside of Prismplex Discord and Minecraft servers are not the responsibility of Prismplex, but if there are reports of a member not being suitable for our community or using our community to do bad things, such as finding victims, it should be reported, and we will try our best to help reasonably or provide better options for receiving help.
