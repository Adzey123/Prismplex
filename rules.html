As a friendly community full of LGBTQIA+ and neurodivergent people, we have rules to keep the server friendly and a safe space for all.

The Basics

  • Don't advertise other servers or your own servers. Self-promotion is allowed (social media such as youtube as an example) but please use common sense and don't spam or send it to unrelated channels.
  • The staff team has the final say over this to keep the server clean.
  • Please keep the contents of the server PG friendly.

In-Game Rules on Azure

  • Harassment, unprovoked PVP, griefing, stealing, client mods that give a large advantage (hacks) and anything else deemed unsuitable by the staff are not allowed on the server. Lightmatica, coordinate overlays and other non-unfair mods are okay.
  • In game on azure please keep chat friendly, dont send stuff you wouldnt say to a 12 year old.
  • If a player is breaking the rules or suspected of cheating, please DM a staff member (such as @zeyzy or @lentex) or message in #general.

Discord Rules

  • Please avoid spam in Discord and Minecraft chats and follow all requests from staff.
  • Please use spoiler tags to censor sensitive topics and provide content or trigger warnings when needed.
  • Our real_talk channel is for serious conversations, so please be sensitive and respectful.
  • Homophobia, racism, sexism, transphobia, and other forms of discrimination are not allowed.

TLDR: Quick Rules

  • No advertising, small amounts of self-promotion allowed.
  • Be kind and use spoilers to censor sensitive content.
  • Keep it PG and don't hack in-game.
  • Avoid any forms of discrimination; otherwise, you may face a ban.

Contact Us

If you have concerns or need to reach a staff member, contact @zeyzy or @lentex.

A Google form to submit requests, complaints, unban requests, and more is available here.

If you are unsure or confused, just ask in #general or DM a staff member.

Staff members make the final decisions, and if you believe a punishment was unjust, reach out to @zeyzy or @lentex, the main staff managing the server and player communication.
