Welcome to Prismplex

Prismplex is a community oriented Minecraft server that is made up of two unique, high quality gameplay options to our players: The Azure, and The Crimson.

Our community consists of a wide variety of friendly players, and we strive to be a safe space for gamers of all identities to have a fun place to play Minecraft and make meaningful friendships.

Chat with Others

The Prismplex is a community server, with conversations taking place in the Discord daily about the Minecraft server as well as various other topics.

See Notifications

Keep up to date on various Prismplex events, such as contests, minigames, elections, as well as other important information!

Use Bots

All Prismplex servers have a bot to give a live view of the server's chat, and allows players on Discord to talk to people in-game without needing to be on the server.

Find Trades

If you are looking for a specific good or service, check out the trades room! You can also advertise items that you are selling yourself and make offers with other players.

Join a Team

Use the room for players looking to join a team on the server or for those that are recruiting. In general, the server is also a great place to meet new people and make friends!
