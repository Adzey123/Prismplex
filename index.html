Welcome to Prismplex

Prismplex is a community oriented Minecraft server that offers two unique, high quality gameplay options to our players: The Azure SMP, and the Crimson Prison.

Our community consists of a wide variety of friendly players, many of which identify with the LGBTQ+ community. We strive to be a safe space for gamers of all identities to have a fun place to play Minecraft and make meaningful friendships.
